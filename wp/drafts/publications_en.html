Courses and tutorials

The creation of educational activities using JClic

Telematic course of 60 hours integrated into the In-service Teacher Training Plan of the Ministry of Education of Catalonia. The teachers registered to the course have the advice of one tutor.

The complete course consists of 3 introductory modules on multimedia, image and digital sound, and 5 specific modules about JClic and JClic Author.

The course is available in Catalan, Spanish, German and English.

Un passeig pel JClic

Pere Cornellà, professor of the IES de Celrà (Catalonia), has created a set of activities that are used for discovering the main characteristics of JClic: Operation of the different modules, elements of the screen, types of activities, etc. The activities are currently only available in Catalan and Spanish

Guia del JClic

Jaume Bartrolí has created this PDF document based on the course The Creation of Educational Activities using JClic about JClic Author and JClic Player. This documentation is only available in Catalan.

Leitfaden zu JClic

Education Highway (Innovationcenter for School and New Technology, Linz) has created a JClic guide in German language for JClic beginners. It explains the creation of JClic activities and the most important features and functions in order to get started with JClic.

The Regionaal Expertisenetwerk (REN), belonging to the Flemish Ministry of Education, hosts different activities related to Clic and JClic. Articles about Clic and JClic

JClic y Edilim: programas de autor para el diseño de actividades educativas en soporte digital para Educación Infantil y Primaria

Raül Tárraga Mínguez, 2012

Article published in number 9 of the magazine “@tic. revista d'innovació educativa" (2012) where the benefits of JClic and Edilim are analyzed, two author programs for the design of educational activities for the stages of early childhood and primary education.

jclic_edilim.pdf

Alfabetización digital del profesorado: herramientas educativas

Francisco Pérez García, 2010

Article published in number 16 of the magazine "Revista DIM: Didáctica, Innovación y Multimedia," (2010) where the importance of improving teacher training in ICT is discussed, specifically in relation to the creation of digital virtual content interactive. In particular, the article refers to the following tools: JClic, Virtual Notebooks, Exelearning, HotPotatoes and Ardora.

alfabetizacion_digital.pdf

El uso de JClic como complemento para la enseñanza/aprendizaje de la Educación Física

Antonio Ariza García y Santiago Romero Granados, 2009

Article published in number 15 of the magazine "Retos nuevos tendencias en educación física, deporte y recreación" (2009) where the use of JClic for the creation of multimedia and interactive didactic applications to work on procedural aspects of various areas of the curriculum is discussed. Specifically, in the design of activities for the introduction of physical education at different levels.

educacion_fisica.pdf

Utilización del programa JClic para el diseño de actividades de geografía en educación primaria y secundaria

Antonio Ariza García, 2008

Article published in number 22 of the magazine "ESPACIO Y TIEMPO, Revista de Ciencias Humanas" (2008) where the importance of new technologies in teaching in general and geography in particular is discussed.

geografia_jclic.pdf

Creatividad y elaboración de materiales didácticos multimedia de naturaleza artesanal para Educación Infantil

José Antonio Ortega Carrillo, 2008

Article published in number 12 of the magazine "Creatividad y sociedad: magazine of the Association for Creativity" (2008) on the role played by teaching creativity, the design and creation of multimedia teaching materials aimed at learning to read in child education.

A un clic de ratón

Daniel López, 1999

Article published in number 157 of the magazine "Comunicaciun y Pedagogía" (March 1999) where the experience of using the Clic in the CP "El Llano" in Monesterio (Badajoz) is explained. Daniel is also the author of various materials from the ZonaClic: Pasarratos, Animaladas, Gusiletras and Las vocales.

aunclic.pdf

Fonaments matemàtics, psicopedagògics i curriculars per a la generació d'activitats de càlcul mental per a l'ensenyament primari amb el mòdul Arith

Jordi Quintana, 1995

The document wants to be a tool for all teachers who want to create mental calculation activities with the Arith module of JClic. It presents a proposal for grading difficulties in addition, subtraction, multiplication and division, duly justified. The proposal can serve both as a model and as a starting point to elaborate a correct sequencing of mental calculation contents, which allows the generation of activities of progressive and cumulative difficulty. Includes a selection of references to mental calculation and the use of information technology.
